## 4. Voice & Tone Guide

**Voice Archetype:** The gentle night-sky curator. Wise, poetic, intimate, never preachy or clinical.

### Core Traits

- Serene but alive (not sleepy or flat)
- Cosmic yet grounded (stars + candlelight)
- Evocative over explanatory
- Second-person intimacy (“you drift…”, “let this carry you…”)
- Soft sensory language: *velvet, drift, glow, horizon, hush, ember, tide*

### Do’s

- Use poetic micro-stories in descriptions
- Mention human curation and post-production proudly but gracefully
- Suggest experience, never promise medical outcomes
- Short paragraphs + breathing room

### Don’ts

- No hype (“ultimate”, “life-changing”, “you must”)
- No medical claims (“reduces cortisol”, “heals anxiety”)
- No emoji spam or clickbait punctuation
- Never sound like a generic AI prompt

### Example YouTube Description Opening

> Under a velvet meridian of stars, low pulses move like slow tides across an infinite hush. Human-curated and finished with care, this soundscape invites the body to soften and the mind to loosen its grip on the day.

### Secondary Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Rose Gold** | `#B76E79` | Subtle warmth in illustrations, hover states |
| **Starlight Cream** | `#F8F4ED` | Body text on dark, soft dividers |

### Usage Rules

- Always maintain high contrast (gold/cream on indigo).
- Use gold accents sparingly — think jewelry, not decoration.
- Subtle radial or linear gradients from indigo to midnight are encouraged.
- Never use pure black or neon. Avoid high-saturation colors entirely.

## 8. Logo Concepts (For Designer)

### Direction 1 — Refined Wordmark (Primary Recommendation)

Pure typography focus. **“LUMEN”** in a custom or Playfair-inspired elegant serif with delicate warm-gold glow treatment on the counter of the “U” or as a soft outer bloom. **“AI MUSIC”** sits centered beneath in a refined, slightly tracking-adjusted sans (Inter or similar). Extremely scalable — works at 24px favicon size and on dark/light. Timeless and ownable.

### Direction 2 — Symbol + Wordmark

A custom abstract symbol sits to the left or above the wordmark: a stylized soft-glowing orb/lantern emitting three very delicate warm-gold light rays that subtly suggest a soundwave or gentle horizon. The symbol feels like a cross between an old observatory lens and a candle flame. Wordmark uses the same elegant serif + sans pairing. The symbol can be used standalone as app icon or watermark.

### Direction 3 — Abstract Mark (Icon-Only)

Standalone icon: soft concentric or overlapping translucent rings in warm gold and rose gold that suggest a gentle light portal, cosmic ripple, or peaceful lens flare. Extremely minimal, works at tiny sizes, and feels premium on merch, app icon, or as a subtle repeating background pattern. No text. Perfect for favicon and loading states.

## 11. Thumbnail System

### Layout Rules (Non-Negotiable)

- Full-bleed cinematic hero image (starfield, rain on window at night, nebula with god rays, velvet dark interior with single light source)
- Dark gradient overlay at bottom 30% for text legibility
- Title in large elegant serif (Playfair), 2–3 lines max, generous letter-spacing
- Subtitle line in clean sans (Inter), smaller weight
- Subtle pill badge bottom-left or top-right with thin gold stroke: `[Deep Sleep]` `[Focus Flow]` `[Cinematic Drift]` `[Nervous System Reset]`
- Watermark: **“Lumen AI Music”** in tiny clean sans, 60–70% opacity, bottom-right corner
- No faces, no arrows, no emojis, no red/yellow clickbait colors

### Title Formula

```
[Poetic Name] | [Mode] • [Duration]
```

**Example:** `Velvet Meridian | Cinematic Drift • 2hr 14min`

### Consistent Visual Language

Always shoot or generate in the same color grade (deep indigo dominant, warm gold light sources). Maintain 16:9 with safe margins.

- “Uninterrupted drift”

Wellness shop feels like a **quiet apothecary** — candles, journals, and objects that match the sonic world. Plenty of negative space. Micro-interactions are gentle fades and soft gold glows on hover.

**Overall feeling:** Observatory at night meets high-end Japanese minimalism meets old vinyl warmth. Slow, intentional, beautiful.

## 14. Brand Do / Don’t List

### DO

- Prioritize negative space and calm
- Credit human curation gracefully
- Use sensory, poetic language
- Test every asset at favicon size
- Maintain consistent color grade across all visuals
- Let the music and imagery breathe

### DON’T

- Use neon, bright, or clinical colors
- Make medical or performance claims
- Employ aggressive marketing language or arrows
- Overcrowd thumbnails or layouts
- Treat AI as the hero (human craft is the hero)
- Use cheap stock imagery or generic AI art styles
- Rush releases — every piece must feel intentional

## 15. Competitor Differentiation

### vs. Generic Ambient YouTube Channels (10-hour rain, brown noise spam)

We are selective, high-production, poetically named, and human-finished. We own the full experience via our streaming site and build a real community of intentional listeners rather than passive autoplay.

### vs. Raw AI Music Channels (direct Suno / Udio dumps)

Every track receives real post-production: editing, mastering, intentional emotional arcs, and optional healing frequency layering. Quantity is never the goal — emotional truth and craft are.

### vs. Wellness Apps (Calm, Headspace, Insight Timer)

We are pure instrumental art, not guided content or stories. Our aesthetic is cinematic and cosmic rather than clinical. We offer a deeper, longer, more immersive artistic experience for people who already know how to be quiet.
